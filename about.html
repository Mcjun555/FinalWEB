All About Educare

Educare College Inc. (formerly Educare International School)

Founded by Dr. Myrna Campos

The school was founded August 2004 and started with just a small community of students not more than fifty. The school accomodated nursery, elementary, and junior high school students (since senior high is not yet implemented). There were only around ten teachers and staff working for the school. During this time, the school only has a five storey building where classrooms, faculty room, and canteen is located.

As the years pass, more students enrolled and the community continuously grow. The board of directors decided to have improvements to the school. Another canteen was made for the students, a service ride was offered to students who wants to be fetched and accompany them from their respective houses.

The construction of a new building was also started for additional calssrooms. This is a six storey building that is ongoing construction up to present. With this building, the school owner's acquaintance introduced a part of this building to be a lodging for his co-Korean students.

At present, the school is considered to be a community of Filipinos and Koreans. A lot of collaborations and connections are being done by the school for the students for better education experience.


School Features

As of today, there are enough classrooms that can accomodate up to 600 students. Although the school grounds is not enough as of the moment, it can be widened as the number of students grow and however it can be seen fit. There are two canteen spaces for the students to have recess breaks and lunch breaks. enough space for students to interact.

The school facilities are also good for use. The computer laboratory has 23 working units that are used by the senior high computer programming students and also by the lower years with their computer subjects. The science laboratory is also used by the students who requires experiments and other laboratory subjects. There are microscopes, beakers, and essential tools for variety of scientific uses. There is also the wide multi-purpose kitchen used by the senior high home economics students as well as junior high students who has cooking or baking subjects.

There are also installed CCTV cameras at hallways, canteens, porch, stairs, and the school grounds. This is to ensure safety of the students and also their security. This is also used to monitor the actions of the students especially those who has love life na nag haharutan sa hallway. Even the students who did nothing wrong, not even display their affection to each other to the public.
