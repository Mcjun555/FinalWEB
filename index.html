MISSION

EDUCARE is dedicated to providing a firm
foundation of knowledge and Filipino values for the
future leaders of the nation. We are committed to
serving the community by offering quality and affordable education.

VISION

EDUCARE has for its objective, the bringing
into being of well-rounded graduates, proficient
in all duties and endeavors required of upstanding,
productive, and socially responsible Filipino citizens.

HIGHLIGHTS

Just like any other schools, Educare also conducts these kinds of competitions, particularly math and science. A group of three participants will battle out their brains. There are two departments in the competition, namely elementary and high school departments. Three students are chosen each grade level to join the math quiz bee, and another three for science quiz bee.






A sportsfest is yearly conducted in the campus. This includes different sports and traditional Filipino games. The highlights of this event would be the pageant and the cheering competition. Students from the 3rd grade up to the 12th are grouped into 4 teams and play against each other. The points accumulated in each event are gathered and the team with the highest points will be the champion.
